# WingStart
MSCH-C220 Final Project

## Theme
When I think of a "one button" game I immediatley think of cookie clicker. I wanted to do something similar and make a game that you can go to just to pass time and have seen many different themes to these type of idle games so I decided to choose something I really like which is Wingstop.

## Game Play
The objective is to click the wing as much as you can and make as much money as possible through upgrades and perks.

## What did you 
Something that surprised me during the implementation was the amount of effects that needed to be implemented in order for the gameplay to feel nice. One of them was easy, which was the click effect on the wing, but the other was a little more difficult to figure out which was the cost multiplier. As the game goes on I did not want the cost of the upgrades to increase by too much for the user to think the next upgrade was unreachable, but also not too little to where the game was too easy to get a lot of money. 

## Implementation
* 5 Upgrades that increase the amount of money made. Their cost increase as you buy them
* 2 perks that increase the effect of your clicks
* All art made by me, including sound effects, sprites, and music.
* An info page that explains a bit about the game and some the upgrades.
* Stats page so you can see how much money you've earned in total, money spent, etc.
* Settings menu with mute/unmute for the music and a restart button

## Special Focus
I think my special focus was art and a bit of mechanics. I think my effort is a 5. I made pretty much every aspect of art in the game. I made the image for every item and the background. I also made sure that the game felt nice, every time the user interacts with something there is some sort of indicator that shows it worked/you interacted with it. The progress throughout the game doesn't feel too hard or too easy in my opinion and had me hooked for good periods during the creation of the game. Many of my friends when testing my game would get hooked as well.
